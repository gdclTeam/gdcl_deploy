Profile of the Company


           Guangdong Jian’an Changsheng Engineering Co.,Ltd., is a State Grade I building construction engineering enterprise and its general contracting qualifications include: General Contracting of Grade I Building Engineering Construction,General Constracting of Grade I Municipal Engineering.Specialized contracting qualifications, i.e, Building Construction Decoration Engineering, Groundwork and Foundation Engineering,Earth and Stone Engineering, along with Steel Structure Engineering and Building Curtain-wall Engineering.Now the Company has a staff of about 400 including various kinds of professional engineering technicians and economical personnel, among of them,over 30 have senior technical titles,over 100 have medium technical titles and more than 60 are project manager at different levels.
         The Company has functional departments including Board of Directors, Board of Supervisors, General Manager Office, General Engineer Office, General Economic Engineer, General Accountant Office, Personnel Dept., Engineering Dept., Quality Safety Dept., Operation Dept., Equipment Dept., Finance Dept. and Project Manager Dept.; it has a large number of workers, and more than 300 sets of various kinds of building construction machinery, road building machinery and equipment. The total value of the projects completed by the Company reaches 300 million yuan per year .
         The company passed the GB/T19001-2008 quality Management System certilication,GB/T24001-2004 Environmental Management System certification,GB/T28001-2011 Occupational Health and Safety Management System certilication,the Guangdong Provincial Department of Construction to achieve the safe production licenses issued.
         It has completed about 1000 projects large or small. The main projects completed in recent years are: residence areas including Yinshi Yayuan, Huanan Mingyu, Rongtai Heting, Xuriwan, Wuzhou Huacheng, Sanhao Mingyuan,Lingnan Shijia,Shiji Mingcheng,Xindai Huayuan,Huiqian Huayuan,Zhouji Haoting ,Bixi Licheng,Shishang Xinyuan,GongYuanDao,JinxiuSiji，Jinyu Huafu,Zhonglian Xingji,Dongqiao Xincun; Plant Lab Building and Dormitory Building of Guanglian Communications Company, Phase I and Phase II of Xu Relay, Jinding Primary School, Senior High School Branch of Doumen No.1 Middle School, Law Service Building, Reconstruction Project of S.Yingbin Road, Dongxing Road, Cuiqian Road, Bitumen Road Surface of Liandao Levee, and Phase Drainage Project of Baijiao Industrial Park; Southern Flood-draining dyke of Baijiao Industry Park, No.1 Bridge of Baijiao Road, Sanban Bridge of Xiaolin, Decoration Project of Shijing Hill Hotel, Decoration Project of Headquarter Building of Lizhu Company, Decoration Project of Comprehensive Building of Yide Plaza, No.1 Plant of Guanglian Communications Company, and Wanzai Duty-free Mall.
         Projects that won Excellent Example Project at Provincial and Municipal level are: Reconstruction Project of S.Yingbin Road, Comprehensive Building of Easton Peach Scientific and Technological Park, Lab Building and Dormitory Building of Guanglian Communication Company, Hengshan Zhongxin Primary School, Main Production Building of Wangwang Food Co., Ltd.; Project that won Double Excellent Example Construction Site for Safe Production and Civilized Construction are: Langqingju, Dormitory Building of Senior High School Branch of Doumen Middle School, Sanhao Mingyuan, Phase II Project of Zhuhai Xu Relay and Plant of Zhuhai Yongbao Plastics Company.
         Rongtai Heting Project constructed by the Company has been highly acclaim among the industry for its strict and effective management measures on high falling. Provincial and Municipal Construction Bureaus specially organized relevant departments to held Special Site Meeting on Preventing High Falling on the construction site, and also organized experts from all parts of Guangdong Province to visit and experience exchange.
         During many years of construction management, we always adhere to the tenet of Quality First, Customer Supreme and Mutual Beneficial. Our company has been highly acclaimed among owners and all social walks for our high quality, fast speed, punctual schedule and considerate warranty service. The honorable titles that the Company won in recent years are: National Creditable Construction Example Unit, Zhuhai Excellent Building Engineering Unit for Safe and Civilized Unit and Reassurance Enterprise for Building Construction and Decoration, etc. It has been offered Creditable Enterprise By Zhuhai Industry and Commerce Administration in a continuous row years.
         Changsheng will, as we did, exert itself in providing quality service and creating long-lasting Changsheng brand!
